Here's a list of what I can do for your pet:

Bathing: Bathing your pet improves skin condition, helps them shed and keeps them clean!
Simple Tidy: This is a light trim of the coat and a clip of hygiene areas, it should be done between full grooms and it keeps your dog's coat in better condition and not matted.
Styling: This includes everything that would happen with bathing and a tidy but the whole dog would be styled according to your request.
Nail Trimming: One of the most important parts of maintenance of your dog. When nails become too long they can break, overgrown and even damage the pads.
